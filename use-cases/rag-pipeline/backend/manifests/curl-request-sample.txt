## Text embedding

  curl --request POST \
  --url http://rag-backend.ml-team:8000/generate_product_recommendations/ \
  --header 'Content-Type: application/json' \
  --data '{
    "text": "Mens V-neck sweater"
  }'


## Multimodal

  curl --request POST \
  --url http://rag-backend.ml-team:8000/generate_product_recommendations/ \
  --header 'Content-Type: application/json' \
  --data '{
    "text": "Mens V-neck sweater",
    "image_uri": "gs://gkebatchexpce3c8dcb-dev-rag-data/flipkart_images/00322e32b4705cfc74d73032c39a44ab_0.jpg"
  }'
